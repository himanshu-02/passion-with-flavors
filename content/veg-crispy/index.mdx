# Veg Crispy Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as capsicum, carrot, onion, and cauliflower), thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons corn flour
- 1 tablespoon rice flour
- 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
- Salt to taste
- Water, as needed
- Oil for deep frying
- Spring onions, chopped, for garnish
- Sesame seeds, for garnish

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Vegetables:**
   - Thinly slice the vegetables and set aside.

2. **Make the Batter:**
   - In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, corn flour, rice flour, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, red chili powder, and salt.
   - Add water gradually to make a thick batter, ensuring there are no lumps.

3. **Coat the Vegetables:**
   - Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat.
   - Dip the sliced vegetables into the batter, ensuring they are well-coated.

4. **Fry the Vegetables:**
   - Fry the coated vegetables in batches until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.

5. **Garnish and Serve:**
   - Garnish with chopped spring onions and sesame seeds.
   - Serve hot as a snack or appetizer, optionally with a side of sweet chili sauce or ketchup.

Enjoy the crispy and flavorful Veg Crispy!
