# Cheesy Garlic Bread on Sandwich Bread Recipe

**Description:** A delicious and easy-to-make cheesy garlic bread using sandwich bread, topped with freshly grated garlic for an extra burst of flavor.

## Ingredients:
- 4 slices of sandwich bread
- 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 2 tablespoons butter, softened
- 2 cloves garlic, grated using a microplane
- 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions:

1. **Preheat the Oven:** Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. **Prepare the Bread:**
   - Place the sandwich bread slices on the prepared baking sheet.

3. **Make the Garlic Butter:**
   - In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, grated garlic, chopped parsley (if using), salt, and pepper.

4. **Add the Toppings:**
   - Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over each bread slice.
   - Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the buttered bread slices.

5. **Bake the Garlic Bread:**
   - Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for about 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges of the bread are golden brown.

6. **Serve:**
   - Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
   - Cut the cheesy garlic bread into halves or quarters and serve warm as a snack or side dish.

Enjoy the cheesy goodness of this quick and tasty garlic bread!
