# Detroit-Style Chicken Pizza 

## Ingredients:
- 1 batch of Detroit-style pizza dough
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shredded or diced
- 1/2 cup capsicum, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Optional: grated Parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes, fresh basil

## Instructions:

1. **Preheat the Oven:** Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a seasoned Detroit-style pizza pan or baking dish in the oven to preheat as well.

2. **Prepare the Dough:**
   - Press the pizza dough into the preheated pan, spreading it evenly to the edges and up the sides.

3. **Add the Toppings:**
   - Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
   - Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
   - Layer the chicken, capsicum, and onion evenly over the cheese.
   - Season with salt and pepper.

4. **Add the Cheese Layer:**
   - Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the toppings.

5. **Bake the Pizza:**
   - Drizzle olive oil over the top of the pizza.
   - Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and caramelized.

6. **Serve:**
   - Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
   - Optional: sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes, and fresh basil before serving.

Enjoy the unique and delicious flavors of this Detroit-style pizza!
