# Mutton Kheema with Bread and Sunny Side-Up Egg

## Ingredients:
- 500g mutton kheema (minced mutton)
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
- 2 green chilies, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
- Salt, to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves, chopped, for garnish
- Bread slices, toasted, for serving
- Eggs, for frying

## Instructions:

1. **Cook the Kheema:**
   - Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
   - Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
   - Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for another minute.
   - Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.

2. **Add Spices:**
   - Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
   - Add the minced mutton (kheema) and mix everything together. Cook for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the kheema is cooked through.

3. **Prepare the Eggs:**
   - In a separate pan, fry the eggs sunny side-up.

4. **Serve:**
   - To serve, place a spoonful of mutton kheema on a plate.
   - Top with a fried egg.
   - Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
   - Serve hot with toasted bread slices.

Enjoy the delicious and hearty Mutton Kheema with Bread and Sunny Side-Up Egg!
