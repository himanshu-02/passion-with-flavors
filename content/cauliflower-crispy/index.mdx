# Cauliflower Crispy 65 Recipe

**Description:** A delicious and crispy cauliflower dish inspired by Chicken 65, coated in chickpea flour and garnished with fried curry leaves and green chilies.

## Ingredients:
- 1 medium cauliflower, cut into florets
- 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
- 2 tablespoons rice flour
- 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt to taste
- Water, as needed
- Oil for frying
- Fresh curry leaves and green chilies, for garnish

## Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Cauliflower:**
   - Blanch the cauliflower florets in hot water for 3-4 minutes. Drain and set aside.

2. **Make the Batter:**
   - In a bowl, combine chickpea flour, rice flour, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
   - Add water gradually to make a thick batter, ensuring there are no lumps.

3. **Coat the Cauliflower:**
   - Dip each blanched cauliflower floret into the batter, ensuring it is well-coated.

4. **Fry the Cauliflower:**
   - Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat.
   - Fry the coated cauliflower florets in batches until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.

5. **Garnish:**
   - In the same oil, fry fresh curry leaves and green chilies until crisp. Use these to garnish the fried cauliflower.

6. **Serve:**
   - Serve the crispy cauliflower 65 hot as a snack or appetizer, optionally with a side of mint chutney or sauce of your choice.

Enjoy the crispy and flavorful Cauliflower Crispy 65!
